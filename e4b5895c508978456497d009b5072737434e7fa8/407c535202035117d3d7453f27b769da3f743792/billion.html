Turn off the billion

Steps:

  1. Go onto the roof of the billion
  2. Find the middle of the roof and gentley push down on the indented square
  3. When the trap door opens get a ladder and hook it onto the edge of the roof
  4. Climb down the ladder and sit on the bottom rung
  5. Put your phone on the ground with the Twyford Official app open
  6. Lift up the phone and three words should appear on the ground where it was
  7. Type the words into the keyboard on the wall making sure not to touch the floor
  8. You now have five minutes until the entire billion turns off leaving all doors in a locked state
Hope you can do it!
